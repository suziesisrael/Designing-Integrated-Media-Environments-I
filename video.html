demonstrating an app functionality

Audio & Video Production

The DiabetesGO! App is an animated, micro-learning curriculum to help people with diabetes manage their health. In this video, I show how I use the DiabetesGO! App to see how well I understand diabetes self-care, and then how I can improve my knowledge by watching the animated modules in the app.

I used Adobe XD to create the screen shots of the app and Adobe Premiere Pro to stitch it all together. The micro-learning video was developed by In-Range Animation, a company my husband and I started in 2019. The calendar sequence was sourced from Vecteezy.com. Music was sourced from Soundstripe.com.
