About Me

I am a web designer, entrepreneur, writer, and artist. I believe everyone deserves to be self-expressed.

I am a web designer at the University of Florida College of Liberal Arts and Sciences (UF CLAS). I also co-own an animation firm with my husband, Neil. I am taking this class to learn how to create effective training videos so my customers at UF can learn the WordPress ecosystem of CLAS. Also, in my animation business I am working on a curriculum for people with diabetes to learn how to manage their health effectively. My educational background is in fine and commercial art, and I have been a web designer for about twenty years. I am here to learn more about how people learn and to make lessons memorable.
